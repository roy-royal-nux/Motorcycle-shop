About Iron & Chrome

Since 1995, Iron & Chrome has been building custom motorcycles for riders who demand more than stock machines. Our master craftsmen combine traditional techniques with modern innovation to create motorcycles that are as beautiful as they are powerful.

We believe every motorcycle tells a story. Whether you're a seasoned rider or dreaming your first custom build, we're here to make it reality. Our team brings decades of combined experience in welding, mechanics, design, and artistry.

Located in the heart of the city with a state-of-the-art workshop, we're committed to quality, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction on every project.
